# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is PolyWorld?
PolyWorld is a virtual land investment platform where you can buy digital representations of real-world cities and earn passive yield.

### How does the yield work?
Your land generates daily yield based on its rarity. The protocol treasury earns from DeFi lending, and those earnings are distributed to landowners.

### Is this a game or an investment?
Both! It's a gamified investment platform. You collect cities like NFTs while earning real yield.

## Technical

### Which blockchain does PolyWorld use?
Solana - for fast transactions and low fees.

### What wallet do I need?
Phantom Wallet is recommended. You can get it at phantom.app

### Is there a mobile app?
Not yet, but the website is mobile-responsive.

## Land & Yield

### How many cities are available?
908 cities across all 7 continents.

### What's the difference between Epic, Rare, and Common?
- Epic (major cities): 4% APY, $500-1000
- Rare (medium cities): 3% APY, $200-400  
- Common (remote areas): 2% APY, $50-150

### Can I sell my land?
Yes, the protocol offers guaranteed buyback with a 5% fee.

### When do I receive yield?
Yield accrues daily and can be claimed anytime.

## Security

### Are the smart contracts audited?
Not yet - currently on devnet for testing. Audit planned before mainnet.

### Is my money safe?
This is experimental software. Only use funds you can afford to lose.

## Getting Started

### How do I start?
1. Install Phantom Wallet
2. Visit tektonxyz.github.io/polyworld/public/
3. Connect your wallet
4. Browse cities and buy land

### Do I need SOL?
Yes, for transaction fees. You can get devnet SOL from the Solana faucet.
