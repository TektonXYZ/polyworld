## рҹҢҚ What is PolyWorld?

PolyWorld is a **gamified virtual land investment platform** where players buy digital parcels of real-world Earth, stake USDC into a treasury, and earn passive yield as "rent" from DeFi earnings.

### рҹҺҜ Core Concept
- **Buy real locations** вҖ” From New York to Antarctica
- **Earn daily yield** вҖ” 2-4% APY based on land rarity  
- **Protocol guarantees** вҖ” Buyback anytime with 5% fee
- **Zero speculation** вҖ” Real DeFi yield, not PvP trading

## рҹ“Ҡ Game Mechanics

### Land Rarity & Yield

| Type | Locations | Price | Daily Yield | APY |
|------|-----------|-------|-------------|-----|
| рҹҸҷпёҸ **EPIC** | Major Cities | $500-1000 | ~$0.55-1.10 | 4% |
| рҹҢІ **RARE** | Medium Cities | $200-400 | ~$0.16-0.33 | 3% |
| рҹҸ”пёҸ **COMMON** | Remote Areas | $50-150 | ~$0.03-0.08 | 2% |

### Treasury Mechanics
- Player deposits вҶ’ Treasury
- Treasury вҶ’ DeFi lending protocols
- Yield вҶ’ Distributed as "rent" to land owners
- Protocol takes 5% fee on sells
